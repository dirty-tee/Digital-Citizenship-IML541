Stay Safe, Stay Smart Online

Protect Your Personal Info

🔒 Don’t share: full name, school name, address, phone number
🔐 Set social media to private
👤 Use nicknames or avatars online

Use Strong Passwords

🔑 Use 8+ characters
🔀 Mix letters, numbers & symbols
🚫 Don’t use your name or birthday

Spotting Scams & Strangers

⚠️ Don’t click on weird links
🚷 Don’t reply to unknown DMs
🤔 Trust your gut if something feels off

If Something Feels Wrong

📢 Tell a trusted adult
🛡️ Block the person
🚫 Report the content
