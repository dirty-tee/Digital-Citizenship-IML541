What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship means being a responsible and respectful user of technology. Just like in real life, we have to follow rules online!

Why Important?

Because everything you do online — from posting a comment to sharing a picture — leaves a mark.

✅ Keep you safe
✅ Protect others
✅ Make the internet a better place for everyone
